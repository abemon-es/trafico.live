## a) Skyscanner Partners

**Owner:** Direct program via Impact.com (Skyscanner manages in-house; not Awin as of 2024)

**Apply URL:** https://app.impact.com/campaign-campaign-info-v2/Skyscanner.brand?io=3GCV1x2n2aiwNz%2BNKwUnnNUJn20aqivVDlZAFuYmnASJwH4LD8cCgEWZv9ljL6%2Bq

**Landing page:** https://www.partners.skyscanner.net/product/affiliates

**Commission:** Revenue share — you earn ~20% of the commission Skyscanner receives from partners (not 20% of the booking price). For a €150 flight where Skyscanner earns €3, you'd earn ~€0.60. Exact rates negotiated within Impact dashboard after approval.

**Cookie:** 30 days

**Approval window:** 1–2 weeks (manual review reported by affiliates)

**Required info:**
- Website URL (must be HTTPS, live)
- Monthly unique visitors — minimum 5,000 (or 1,000 followers for social/influencer)
- Traffic source breakdown
- Content focus description
- Tax info + bank account (set up in Impact.com after approval)

**Common rejection reasons:**
- Traffic below 5,000 UVs/month (mitigator: submit with launch traffic projections + sitemap count; emphasize 27k indexable pages)
- Content not travel-focused (mitigator: our content is exclusively transport)
- Low-quality or thin content (mitigator: we have 150+ SSR pages with real-time data)

**Payout:** Monthly via Impact.com (PayPal or bank transfer), min ~$50

**Tracking options:** Deep links, banners, widgets, text links via Impact dashboard. Travel API available for high-traffic partners (apply separately).

**Network note:** Skyscanner Australia/NZ is on Awin. Spain/global program is on Impact.com directly. If the Impact URL above changes, go to `partners.skyscanner.net` → "Affiliate programme."

**Pitch template (paste in "why join" field):**
- trafico.live is Spain's largest real-time multimodal transport intelligence platform, covering roads, railways, ferries, flights, and public transit for 27,000+ indexable entity pages
- Our aviation page (`/aviacion`) tracks live aircraft positions over Spain and profiles 42 AENA airports — users researching flights naturally convert to Skyscanner searches
- We have a Spanish-first audience of commuters, frequent flyers, and fleet managers actively planning travel
- Our launch (April 2026) is backed by structured SEO covering all major Spanish cities, provinces, and transport hubs
- We operate fully server-side-rendered pages with no cookie consent blockers on our affiliate redirects

**If rejected:** Apply to Travelpayouts (they have a Skyscanner tool as a sub-affiliate network — lower traffic requirements)

**1Password checklist:**
```
[ ] Impact.com login email
[ ] Impact.com password
[ ] Skyscanner Partners affiliate ID (post-approval)
[ ] Deep link base URL
[ ] Tax ID (NIF Certus SPV, SLU)
[ ] Bank account for payments
```

## b) Trainline Partners

**Owner:** Direct program managed on Partnerize network. CRITICAL — Trainline resells Renfe, OUIGO, Iryo, Eurostar, SNCF, etc.

**Apply URL:** https://join.partnerize.com/trainline/en

**Contact:** affiliates@thetrainline.com

**Commission:** 3.5% of booking for new customers / 0.5% for returning customers. CPA rev share. Spain focus: Renfe/OUIGO/Iryo bookings go through Trainline's booking engine and qualify.

**Cookie:** 30 days

**Approval window:** 1–2 weeks (manual review by affiliate team)

**Required info:**
- Website URL
- Content description (blogs, comparison sites, cashback, deals all accepted)
- Promotional methods you'll use
- Estimated traffic (no published minimum, but "relevant audience" required)
- No joining fee
- Tax ID + bank/PayPal set up in Partnerize after approval

**Common rejection reasons:**
- Unclear content strategy (mitigator: emphasize our real-time train data at `/trenes` with 115 live GPS trains)
- Thin site at time of application (mitigator: apply post-launch April 20, after content is live)
- Promoting brand terms via SEM (we don't do SEM — mention this)

**Payout:** Monthly via Partnerize, min ~$50, bank transfer or PayPal

**Tracking options:** Deep links (per-destination), banners, widgets via Partnerize dashboard; CPA and tenancy models available

**Pitch template:**
- trafico.live runs Spain's most comprehensive real-time rail intelligence platform — `/trenes` tracks ~115 live LD trains by GPS and ranks Renfe brands by punctuality
- Our `/trenes/estaciones` catalogs 2,154 stations; `/trenes/lineas` covers 1,248 routes — every station/route page will link to Trainline booking
- We reach the underserved segment of travelers who first check real-time delays, then convert to booking — a natural funnel from our delay dashboards
- Renfe, OUIGO, and Iryo are all Trainline-bookable operators; our pages are natively structured around those same operators
- Content is in Spanish, SEO-structured, server-side rendered — optimized for Spanish rail traveler intent

**If rejected:** Rail Europe affiliate program (https://www.raileurope.com/en/affiliate) — also covers Spanish operators, ~4% commission, Impact.com

**1Password checklist:**

## c) DirectFerries Connect

**Owner:** Direct program (directferriesconnect.com — B2B partner platform). Covers Baleàrics, Canarias, Ceuta/Melilla, UK, Mediterranean routes.

**Apply URL:** https://ww2.directferries.com/affiliate/partner_sign_up.aspx?stdc=DF10

**Info page:** https://www.directferriesconnect.com/

**Contact:** partnerships@directferries.com

**Commission:** 50% of the commission DirectFerries receives from ferry operators. In practice this translates to roughly 1.5–2% of booking value. "For every confirmed ferry booking (for a ferry that sailed)."

**Cookie:** Not published — needs confirmation post-login to partner portal

**Approval window:** Hours to several weeks (reportedly fast for well-described applications)

**Required info:**
- Website URL + description
- Expected booking volume / audience size
- Business type (content site, comparison, travel agent)
- No joining fee reported
- Bank details added post-approval

**Common rejection reasons:**
- Vague application (mitigator: specify exact ferry routes covered — Baleàrics, Canarias, Ceuta — and how our maritime page at `/maritimo` contextualizes ferry demand)
- B2C affiliate vs. B2B travel agent framing (pick affiliate/affiliate tracking links, not travel agent portal)

**Payout:** Per-booking settlement tracked in real-time dashboard; payment schedule not publicly disclosed — confirm with partnerships@directferries.com post-approval. Minimum not published.

**Tracking options:** Affiliate tracking links, booking widgets, Connect API, white-label option

**Pitch template:**
- trafico.live covers Spanish maritime transport including AIS vessel tracking, Puertos del Estado port catalog (197 Spanish ports), and Baleàrics/Canarias ferry routes — `/maritimo` is a full maritime hub
- Our users are trip-planning travelers who check ferry schedules before booking — ferry affiliate links are a direct conversion path
- We track Fred. Olsen, Baleària, and Vizcaya ferry routes with real-time GTFS data — DirectFerries covers exactly the same operators
- SEO pages for Baleàrics, Canarias, and mainland ferry routes will place affiliate calls-to-action at the point of intent
- Spanish-language, SSR content — no paywalls, no cookie consent interruptions

**If rejected:** Aferry.com affiliate program (Commission Junction/CJ Affiliate, ~2% commission, covers similar Spanish routes)

**Cookie:** 60 days

**Approval window:** Days to 2 weeks after Awin account is approved

**Cookie:** 30 days (typical for CPL programs)

**Required info for both:**

## e) Awin (Affiliate Network — Publisher Account)

**Why apply:** Awin is the gateway to FlixBus (ES, DE, global), Renfe's future program, Vueling, Iberia Express, Alsa, and dozens of other Spanish travel brands. Essential to have this account before applying to any Awin-hosted merchants.

**Apply URL:** https://ui.awin.com/publisher-signup/en/awin/step1

**Cost:** £5 / €5 registration deposit (refunded when you track your first sale or reach first payment threshold; refundable if rejected)

**Approval window:** 5–10 business days (compliance review)

**Requirements:**
- Live, HTTPS website (must be publicly accessible at time of review)
- Age 18+
- Valid bank account (deposit required)
- Website content must be described accurately during signup
- No pure incentive/cashback sites without declaring as such

**Common rejection reasons:**
- Site not live at review time (mitigator: apply after April 20 launch, or ensure landing pages are up)
- Incomplete promotional method description (mitigator: describe as "content / comparison / transport data platform")
- Spam-looking site or auto-generated content (mitigator: our pages are rich, SEO-structured, original data)

**Payout:** Monthly, $20 minimum threshold. Direct deposit, PayPal, or wire transfer.

**Travel merchants available on Awin (examples):**
Vueling, Iberia Express, Ryanair (ES), FlixBus (ES/DE/global), Alsa, Interrail/Eurail, Renfe (if/when live), Booking.com, hotels.com

**Pitch template (for "describe your site" field during signup):**
- Content/comparison/data platform for Spanish multimodal transport (roads, rail, ferries, aviation, transit)
- 150+ server-side rendered pages covering all 52 Spanish provinces + major transport entities
- Audience: road commuters, rail passengers, ferry travelers, frequent flyers — all in active trip-planning mode
- Traffic model: organic SEO (no PPC, no incentive), editorial approach, real-time transport data

**If rejected:** Impact.com (free to join, no deposit — apply as backup; hosts Skyscanner, Omio, and others directly)

## Application Order (90-minute session)

Run in this order — each step uses the previous one:

1. **Awin** (20 min) — Apply first. £5 deposit. Required for FlixBus. Wait ~5–10 days.
2. **Rakuten Advertising** (10 min) — Free, instant. Apply immediately after Awin.
3. **Skyscanner via Impact.com** (15 min) — Fill application on Impact. Needs site URL + traffic context.
4. **Trainline via Partnerize** (15 min) — join.partnerize.com/trainline/en. Simplest form.
5. **DirectFerries Connect** (15 min) — Short form + email to partnerships@directferries.com.
6. **FlixBus via Awin** (10 min) — Requires Awin account (submitted in step 1). Search "FlixBus ES" in Awin marketplace and apply to merchant. + BlaBlaCar Bus via Kwanko (5 min).

**Total: ~90 minutes**
