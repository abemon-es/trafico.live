## 6. Local SEO — Provinces and Cities

### Priority City Clusters

**Madrid** (highest volume, toughest competition)
- Weather: `el tiempo Madrid`, `temperatura Madrid ahora`, `lluvia Madrid mañana`
- Fuel: `gasolineras baratas Madrid`, `precio gasolina Madrid`, `gasolineras 24 horas Madrid capital`
- AQ: `calidad aire Madrid hoy`, `NO2 Madrid tiempo real`, `ZBE Madrid qué coches`, `protocolo contaminación Madrid`
- Strategy: Dedicated `/clima/madrid`, `/gasolineras/madrid`, `/calidad-aire/madrid` with live widgets

**Barcelona**
- Weather: `el tiempo Barcelona`, `radar lluvia Barcelona`
- Fuel: `gasolineras baratas Barcelona`, `precio diésel Barcelona hoy`
- AQ: `ZBE Barcelona`, `calidad aire Barcelona PM2.5`, `restricciones coche Barcelona`
- Strategy: ZBE/AQ angle is strongest differentiator — competitors lack combined coverage

**Valencia**
- Weather: `tiempo Valencia`, `DANA Valencia alerta` (peaked post-Oct 2024)
- Fuel: `gasolineras Valencia precio`
- AQ: `calidad aire Valencia`, `contaminación Valencia NO2`
- Opportunity: DANA brand recognition still high; transport+weather disaster prevention angle

**Sevilla, Málaga, Bilbao, Zaragoza, Murcia** — M-volume city pages with all three verticals on one URL (`/localidad/[city]` or dedicated CCAA hubs)

### Province-Level SEO
52 province pages at `/provincia/[slug]` already cover traffic; extend with:
- Province weather widget (AEMET data already collected)
- Province fuel average price ranking
- Province air quality ICA index

### Coastal Clusters (maritime + weather)
`estado del mar [costa]` queries for Costa Brava, Costa del Sol, Costa Cantábrica, Costa Valenciana, Canarias, Baleares — align with `/maritimo` section.
