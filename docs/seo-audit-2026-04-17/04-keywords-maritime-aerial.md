## 3. Branded Competitor Analysis

### Maritime

| Competitor | Ranking Queries | What They Have | What We Have / Could Have |
|---|---|---|---|
| **MarineTraffic** | seguimiento buques, AIS España, posición barco, rastreo buques, tráfico Estrecho | Global AIS network, 800K+ vessel positions/day, vessel history, port calls, premium API, satellite AIS. DA ~70, brand dominance | Embedded AIS map on /maritimo, contextual Spanish framing, bilingual SEO, integration with port stats and weather |
| **VesselFinder** | posición barco mapa, AIS barcos gratis, rastreo barcos | Clean UI, free tracking, 500K vessel database, photo gallery, port details. DA ~55 | Ferry-specific overlays, regional port pages, Spanish administrative hierarchy (CC.AA.) |
| **Directferries.es / Clickferry.com** | ferry a Mallorca precio, ferry Baleares, ferry a Canarias | Full booking engine, price comparison, operator matrix, route finder | Real-time ferry tracking + weather overlay, route context, departure/arrival boards |
| **AEMET (aemet.es)** | meteorología marítima, oleaje España, estado del mar | Official data source, text + map forecasts, 48h + 5-day | Visual, interactive, mobile-first map with real-time buoy data (AEMET as data source) |
| **Puertos del Estado** | oleaje tablas, viento puertos, estadísticas portuarias | Official data, AEMET partnership, port wave tables | Aggregated view with maritime weather + vessel positions in one UI |

**Gap summary:** MarineTraffic and VesselFinder control vessel tracking. Ferry booking is dominated by Directferries/Clickferry/Balearia. Maritime weather is owned by AEMET. trafico.live's angle is contextual aggregation — vessel data + port stats + maritime weather + ferries on one page, with Spanish SEO.

### Aerial

| Competitor | Ranking Queries | What They Have | What We Have / Could Have |
|---|---|---|---|
| **FlightRadar24** | radar aviones, seguimiento vuelo, FlightRadar España, aviones sobre España | Global ADS-B network, 200K+ flights/day, 3D view, playback, push alerts, app. DA ~80 | OpenSky-powered map on /aviacion, embedded in Spanish context |
| **FlightAware** | estado vuelo número, llegadas aeropuerto, FlightAware España | Flight status API, airline operations view, predictive ETA, historical flight data | Spanish airport pages with live boards + AENA context |
| **AENA (aena.es)** | infovuelos, salidas aeropuerto Madrid, llegadas Barcelona | Official operator, real timetable data, 63 airports in Spain | Could embed AENA data on per-airport pages, provide richer visualisation |
| **Aeropuertomadrid-barajas.com / aeropuertobarcelona-elprat.com** | aeropuerto Madrid salidas, aeropuerto Barcelona llegadas | Thin informational sites, poor UX, strong keyword domains | Better UX, richer data integration, breadcrumb + flight status |
| **AESA / seguridadaerea.gob.es** | normativa drones España, NOTAM España, registro drones | Official drone regulation authority | Guide content with AESA data + ENAIRE airspace context |

**Gap summary:** Flight tracking (FR24/FlightAware) and official boards (AENA) dominate transactional queries. Thin airport info sites rank on brand+keyword domains. trafico.live can rank on airport + "vuelos hoy" queries with proper per-airport pages (42 AENA airports already in our catalog). Drone regulation is an uncontested informational cluster.
